Bloody Mary

Ingredients:

  • Vodka 45 ml
  • Tomato Juice 120 ml
  • Lemon Juice 10 ml
  • Worchestershire Sauce 10 ml
  • Hot Sauce 2 dashes
  • Crushed Ice 100 ml

Steps:

  1. On a cold glass, put the ice
  2. Mix the tomato and lemon juice, vodka, Worchestershire Sauce
  3. Pour the mix into the glass, put the Hot Sauce drops and stir
  4. Now it is ready to be drinked!
  5. ENJOY
